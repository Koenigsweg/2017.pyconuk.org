PYCON UK

PyCon UK is your community-led volunteer-run conference

Thursday 26th to Monday 30th October 2017
Cardiff City Hall

Register now

PyCon UK is the annual gathering of the UK Python community and its friends from around the world. This year, we return to Cardiff City Hall, from Thursday 26th to Monday 30th October.

There will be a heavyweight boxing match in Cardiff over the weekend of the conference, and so hotel accommodation in the city has been snapped up much more quickly this year than usual. Please see the accommodation page for more details.

The first four days of the conference will include talks and workshops, while the fifth will be a day of sprints.

Tickets are on sale, and applications are open for the Django Girls workshop to be held on the Thursday of the conference.

We're delighted that the Education Track returns, in partnership with the Raspberry Pi Foundation, and we look forward to welcoming teachers and children to the conference.

We're also working with PyData London to put on a track of talks and workshops aimed at data scientists.

It's very important to us that all members of the community feel welcome and comfortable at the conference. Read about our approach to diversity, accessibilty, and inclusion.

We've got many more announcements to make over the coming weeks, so you should sign up to our mailing list.

If you've got any questions, get in touch.

We look forward to seeing you in October!
